Pricing questions

What's included in each plan?

Every plan includes the Excel dashboard tutorials. Paid plans add the full template library, HR and analytics tools, automated content workflows and approval-gated TikTok publishing — scaling up by the number of connected accounts and the level of support.

How do I pay and get started?

You start the conversation on WhatsApp. We confirm the right plan for your business, set up your account, and connect your TikTok with your authorization — nothing is published without your approval.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes. Move up or down whenever your needs change, and cancel anytime. There are no long-term contracts and no setup fees.

Do you offer custom or agency setups?

The Agency plan covers multiple client workspaces and custom dashboards. If you need something specific, message us on WhatsApp and we'll tailor a setup to fit.
